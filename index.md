## About Me

Welcome! I am a final-year PhD student at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am very fortunate to be advised by Dr. [May Dongmei Wang](https://miblab.bme.gatech.edu/). My research interests lies in the interdisciplinary areas between clinical health and machine learning. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Beijing Normal University.

## Research Interests

- **Large Language Models for Clinical Health:** LLMs for Clinical Shared Decision Making, LLMs for Rare Diseases, LLMs for Drugs
- **Explainable Artificial Intelligence:** Explainable Medical Image-based Diagnosis, Adversarial Patch Attacks on Medical Images, Clinical Dataset Understanding
- **Clinical Informatics:** FHIR-based Multi-Site and Multi-Modality Patient Rehabilitation
- **Precision Medicine:** Docking Score Prediction of Drugs
