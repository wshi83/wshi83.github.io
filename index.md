## About Me

Welcome! I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am very fortunate to be advised by Dr. [May Dongmei Wang](https://miblab.bme.gatech.edu/). My research interest lies in the interdisciplinary areas between clinical decision support and machine learning. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Science and Technology at Beijing Normal University.

## Research Interests

- **Large Language Models for Clinical Health:** LLM Agents for Clinical Decision Support
- **Responsible Artificial Intelligence:** Explainable, Trustworthy, Generalized, Fair Models for Translational Clinical Practice
- **Clinical Informatics:** FHIR-based Multi-Site and Multi-Modality Patient Data Integration
- **Precision Medicine:** Generative AI for Personalized Clinical Decision Making
